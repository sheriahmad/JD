May 3, 2004

Jet Parts Engineering was honored as Small Business of the Year by Delta Airlines

Jet Parts Engineering was honored as Small Business of the Year for its consistent track record in providing on-time deliveries of aircraft parts and for cost-savings efforts.

"Delta is committed to increasing the diversity of its suppliers by maximizing the opportunities to purchase goods and services from small, minority-owned and women-owned businesses," said Lamar Chesney, Delta's senior vice president - Supply Chain Management. "By tapping into the innovation, creativity, and commitment to excellence demonstrated by these businesses, Delta continues to create a more inclusive corporate environment and strengthen relationships with the broad range of customers and communities we serve."

Jet Parts Engineering Inc. (JPE) was founded in 1994. JPE supplies parts to many of the worlds' airlines and MRO shops and has earned an outstanding reputation as a leader in the PMA industry. The company's mission is to provide customers with the safest, most reliable parts on the market, and to supply them at a price and speed unmatched by the competition. For more information, please visit the JPE website at www.jetpartsengineering.com.
